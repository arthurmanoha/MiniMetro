2D train game

Square grid
One cell is either empty or a railroad section.
Locomotives and carriages are one cell wide.
A train is made of one locomotive and any number of carriages; it will span over several cells.
Loco and carriage are subclasses of TrainElement.
A loco stores its position and speed, and the ID of its current cell.
A carriage stores its passenger max and current capacity.
A cell with a railroad section may be an intersection with one track splitting into 2 or 3, or merging - depending on the train direction.
A train station is made of one or many cells of type Station.
All Station cells from a given Station have the same Station ID.
A station cell may host passengers.
A passenger has a station ID target.

Tools:

Create stations.
Create passengers with destination.
Create tracks with the mouse.

High-Speed trains:
Go faster than other trains.
Need more track in a straight line to accelerate.
Has to slow down before turns.
